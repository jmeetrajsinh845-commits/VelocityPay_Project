# Dashboard Guide

# Overview

The VelocityPay Analytics Platform includes seven interactive Power BI dashboards designed for different business stakeholders such as executives, finance managers, operations teams, customer success teams, loan managers, and product managers.

Each dashboard focuses on a specific business domain while remaining connected through a common customer data model.

---

# Dashboard 1 — Executive Overview Dashboard

## Purpose

Provide a high-level summary of overall business performance.

## Key KPIs

- Total Customers
- Total Revenue
- Total Transactions
- Payment Success Rate
- Customer Churn Rate

## Visuals

### Monthly Revenue Trend

Shows revenue growth over time.

Business Question Answered

- Is revenue increasing or decreasing?

---

### Transaction Status Distribution

Displays the percentage of:

- Success
- Failed
- Pending
- Refunded

Business Question Answered

- Are payment operations healthy?

---

### Revenue by Industry

Compares revenue generated from each industry.

Business Question Answered

- Which industries generate the highest revenue?

---

## Slicers

- State
- Industry Segment
- Business Type

---

## Business Value

Executives can monitor company-wide performance from a single dashboard.

---

# Dashboard 2 — Customer Churn Dashboard

## Purpose

Identify customer churn patterns and revenue impact.

## Key KPIs

- Total Customers
- Churned Customers
- Churn Rate
- Monthly Revenue Lost

---

## Visuals

### Customer Churn by Industry

Compares churn across industries.

Business Question

Which industries are losing the most customers?

---

### Top Churn Reasons

Displays the most common reasons for customer cancellation.

Examples

- Poor Customer Support
- Product Missing Features
- Better Deal Elsewhere
- Settlement Delays
- Integration Issues

Business Question

Why are customers leaving?

---

### Revenue Loss due to Churn

Shows monthly revenue lost because of churn.

Business Question

How much money is lost due to churn?

---

### Churn Rate by Industry

Ranks industries based on churn percentage.

Business Question

Which industries require immediate retention efforts?

---

## Business Value

Helps customer success teams reduce churn through targeted retention strategies.
